## 📖 About the Project

**Reboot Eco** is a mobile application designed to promote responsible electronic waste disposal within school communities.

The platform transforms public schools into collection hubs where students, teachers, and local residents can donate unused electronic devices and receive **ECO credits** as rewards.

The project aims to combine environmental awareness, community engagement, and gamification to reduce improper electronic waste disposal.

---

## 🌎 The Problem

Electronic waste is one of the fastest-growing waste streams worldwide.

Improper disposal can cause:

- Soil contamination
- Water pollution
- Environmental damage
- Loss of recyclable materials
- Public health risks

Many people are also unaware of where and how to dispose of electronic devices correctly.

---

## 💡 The Solution

Reboot Eco provides a simple and engaging way to recycle electronic devices by:

- Connecting users to nearby collection points
- Registering electronic waste donations
- Tracking recycling activities
- Rewarding users with ECO credits
- Encouraging sustainable behavior through gamification

## 🚀 Main Features

### ♻️ Donation System

- Electronic device registration
- Collection point selection
- Delivery scheduling
- QR Code validation

### 📍 Nearby Collection Points

- View participating schools
- Access location information
- Find nearby collection hubs

### 🔍 Donation Tracking

- Track donation status
- View donation history

### 💰 ECO Wallet

- Check ECO balance
- View transaction history
- Manage rewards

### 🎁 Rewards System

Users can exchange ECO credits for:

- Educational benefits
- Cultural activities
- Partner rewards
- Special campaigns

### 👤 User Profile

- Personal information
- Participation history
- Environmental impact statistics
